About Proper Automotive

Proper Automotive is a full-service automotive workshop dedicated to delivering high-quality mechanical care, trusted workmanship, and exceptional customer service. With experienced technicians, advanced diagnostic equipment, and a commitment to transparency, we provide everything needed to keep your vehicle running safely, reliably, and efficiently.


Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine automotive care through honesty, expertise, and attention to detail. Whether you're maintaining a family car, managing a business fleet, or preparing a vehicle for roadworthy inspection, we ensure every service meets industry standards and exceeds customer expectations.


What We Do

At Proper Automotive, we provide a complete range of automotive services designed to cover all areas of vehicle care, safety, and performance. From basic maintenance to advanced repairs, we are equipped to handle all makes and models with precision and professionalism.


  • General Mechanical Repairs – Brakes, suspension, steering, cooling systems, electrical components, and more.
  • Logbook & Scheduled Servicing – Manufacturer-approved servicing that keeps your warranty valid.
  • Roadworthy & Safety Inspections – Thorough assessments to ensure your vehicle meets VIC road safety standards.
  • Diagnostic Scanning – Advanced computer diagnostics to identify issues quickly and accurately.
  • Transmission Services – Repairs, fluid replacement, and maintenance for both automatic and manual transmission systems.
  • Tyres & Wheel Services – New tyres, balancing, alignments, puncture repairs, and wheel inspections.
  • Battery Testing & Replacement – Fast and reliable replacements with high-quality batteries.
  • Performance Tuning – Improve engine efficiency, power, and fuel economy (optional service).
  • LPG Repairs & Servicing – Inspection, maintenance, and certification of LPG fuel systems.
  • Cooling System & Radiator Repairs – Keep your engine running at the correct temperature.
  • Air Conditioning Services – Re-gassing, leak testing, and full air-con repairs.
  • Fleet Maintenance – Reliable, fast, and cost-efficient servicing for business and commercial vehicles.
