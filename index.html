Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my car making a strange noise?

Noises often indicate mechanical issues such as worn belts, loose components, or brake problems. We can diagnose the cause quickly.

How often should I service my car?

Most vehicles require servicing every 10,000–15,000 km or every 6 months, depending on driving conditions.

Do you provide roadworthy certificates?

Yes — we offer full roadworthy inspections to ensure your car meets Australian safety standards.

Can I get a quote before booking?

Absolutely. Call us anytime for a free estimate based on your car’s needs.
